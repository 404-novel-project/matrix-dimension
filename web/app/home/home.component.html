An alternative integrations manager for Riot

Dimension is a way for people hosting Riot to expand their integrations offering to their users.



Point your Riot config.json to Dimension to start using it:

        "integrations_ui_url": "https://dimension.t2bot.io/riot",
        "integrations_rest_url": "https://dimension.t2bot.io/api/v1/scalar",
        "integrations_widgets_urls": ["https://dimension.t2bot.io/widgets"],


Learn more on Github. Talk about it in #dimension:t2l.io
